# ML Foundations & Neural Networks

## ML Foundations

**Machine Learning Specialization** (Andrew Ng / DeepLearning.AI)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vStJoetOxJg&list=PLkDaE6sCZn6FNC6YRfRQc_FbeQrF8BwGI
The standard starting point for understanding ML. Covers supervised, unsupervised, and neural networks.

**Machine Learning for Everybody – Full Course** (freeCodeCamp)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i_LwzRVP7bg
Beginner-friendly, covers the fundamentals in one go.

**ML Foundations for AI Engineers (34 min)**
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BUTjcAjfMgY
Quick overview of what ML knowledge you actually need as an AI engineer vs. a researcher.

**MLU-Explain** (AWS)
https://mlu-explain.github.io/
Visual, interactive explanations of ML concepts. Great for building intuition.

**fast.ai**
https://www.fast.ai/
Practical-first PyTorch and deep learning. Starts with working code and works backwards to theory.

**Made With ML** (GokuMohandas)
https://github.com/GokuMohandas/Made-With-ML
Production ML — design, train, deploy, and monitor ML systems the right way.

**End-to-End Machine Learning Project – AI, MLOps** (freeCodeCamp)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o6vbe5G7xNo
Full build from raw data to deployed model, covering the MLOps lifecycle — experiment tracking, model registry, CI/CD, and monitoring.

## Neural Networks

**Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** (Andrej Karpathy — playlist)
https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ
Build neural networks from scratch. Starts with micrograd (backprop), works up to GPT. One of the best ML resources ever made.

**The spelled-out intro to neural networks and backpropagation: building micrograd** (Karpathy)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMj-3S1tku0
Single video, two hours. Builds a tiny autograd engine from scratch. Understand this and backprop clicks permanently.

**PyTorch Deep Learning** (mrdbourke)
https://github.com/mrdbourke/pytorch-deep-learning
Zero to mastery PyTorch course — one of the most complete free resources for learning PyTorch.
